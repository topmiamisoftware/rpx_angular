Set Up Your Loyalty Points.

In order to set up your account begin by signing up. We require most of our business accounts to have a well reviewed Yelp or Google Business profile before completing their onboarding process. When you confirm your interest in our platform, we'll schedule an intro call and go over the basics for your business portal along with your Loyalty Points and Rewards list. We'll also give you a quick look around at what our user's see when driving by or walking by your store and what you can expect from us in the near future.

Post Up Some Swag

Every business begins by completing the onboarding process. After the process is completed, you will receive an onboarding package from SpotBie indicating the next steps. Our care package includes various accessories which will help you as a business owner and your clients and seamless experience while claiming Rewards or Loyalty Points.

Start the Show!

After you are all set in and ready, we'll start sending SpotBie Users directly to your store on the days you've chosen as part of your introductory boost promotion.
