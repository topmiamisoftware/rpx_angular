Deleting your Account

In order to delete your SpotBie account you'll need to follow these steps:

  1. Log-in with a personal or business account.
  2. Navigate to the main menu on the top left of your screen.
  3. Tap on Settings.
  4. Scroll to the bottom of the settings section and tap or click on "Deactivate Account"
  5. You will be prompted for your password.
  6. Input your password, tap submit, and your account will be deleted.

Your data will be retained for 3 months during which time you will still be able to recover your account. After these 3 months pass by, your account and data will be permanently deleted.
