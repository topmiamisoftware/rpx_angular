What problem does SpotBie solve?

Loyalty programs are well known to bring in additional revenue and brand awareness for businesses. Many large franchises and retails have in-house loyalty programs. However, some restaurants and retail stores are left with less options to compete because they lack the funding for maintaining an in-house loyalty program.


Alternatively, some businesses use third party loyalty programs that come integrated with POS systems such as Square. While this is a great option, it leaves out the focus on building a highly rich loyalty program experience for your users because of their broad focus.


By signing up through our website, you are becoming part of an unforgettable experience. Not only do we provide an integration for businesses to use on their online stores, but we also offer a stand-alone user app on iOS and Android.

Growing Brand Awareness

SpotBie gives businesses an additional tool to grow their brand. Business owners can use SpotBie to deliver their product/service to the right audience, to be listed amongst relevant/high standard businesses, and appear in users' local and site-wide results.

1. Deliver to the Right Audience

When you are listed on our platform, your business starts attracting users that are one step away from making a decision and start spending. The intent each user has when they open the SpotBie app is to look for something that is nearby and solve their dinner or shopping plans. Quick and easy, at the tap of three buttons (sometimes two), the user is provided with a list of highly rated and reviewed restaurants or retail stores.

2. Listed Amongst Excellence

At SpotBie, you are listed amongst competent restaurants and retails stores that make excellence a habit. To being with, we take a look at ratings and reviews from Yelp. If we consider a business is worth taking a closer look at, we go ahead and experience their food, products, or services through personal consumption. Yes... that's right, we try them out ourselves! We love delicious food, great customer service, and great products so we aren't willing to offer our users anything less!

3. Local Results

Your restaurant or retail store will appear in a map drawn according to the user's distance from your store. In turn, this will ramp-up your local brand awareness near your stores' locations. In the other hand, if there are tourists nearby, our platform simplifies the user experience so that they can easily see what they have around and closest to them.

4. Site-wide Results

Your restaurant, store, service, or event will also be featured on our site-wide results. This means that people from all over the nation can search for your store online and make orders through your website while accumulating loyalty.

Acquisition & Retention

Loyalty programs offer a huge incentive for guests to visit your store. Here at SpotBie, we've added an extra twist that helps bring new customers to your store and retain existing ones.

1. Local Results

Our app allows users to instantly find what stores are around them. Once they open the SpotBie app and click on the category they are searching for, they are presented with a list of nearby restaurants or stores. This makes it easy for users to choose a store, nearing the gap between the prospective user and the end of the marketing funnel.

2. Different Loyalty Campaigns

To keep things entertaining for your customers, we have included the ability to launch different types of campaigns. You can choose to award items, discounts, experiences, and run programs such as birthday awards and loyalty tiers.

3. Compete Locally with other Members

By adjusting your Dollar-to-Loyalty Point ratio you can adjust the value of loyalty points when users spend at your business. Users that look at these businesses thorugh our map or online can then compare reward rates and rewards to see where they want to shop.

4. Set Limits

Business owners can set the minimum expense amounts before rewards can be redeemed at their stores. Additionally, SpotBie Community Members get to choose how much they want to give out in rewards every month.

5. Site-wide Results

SpotBie also allows users to find site-wide stores through the search function on our website.

6. API & Widget Integrations

Do you have an online store and wish to add a loyalty program feature into it? SpotBie can make that happen for you.
