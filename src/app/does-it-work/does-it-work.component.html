What problem does SpotBie solve?

Loyalty programs are well known to bring in additional revenue for businesses. Many franchises and retails own their own in-house loyalty program. However, smaller restaurants and stores are left with less options to compete because they lack the funding for maintaining an in-house loyalty program. SpotBie is a solution for small to medium businesses who are looking to build a brand, grow their crowd, and create loyalty in their community.

Our Vision

We started SpotBie to build a highly rich experience for the people who love the products and services our community members offer and for the ones who provide them; YOU, the business owner.

Creating a Community

Building a loyal audience is a key parameter to your business being successful. With a loyal audience you are almost guaranteed to keep an active flow of revenue. Our Loyalty Points system rewards users for such behavior and gives them additional reasons to visit your store.

Listed amongst Excellence

At SpotBie, you are listed amongst competent restaurants and retails stores who make excellence a habit. We look at ratings and reviews from major platforms and take a closer look at their products and services through personal consumption and use... Yes... we try them out ourselves! We love delicious food, great customer service, and great products. We aren't willing to offer our users anything less!

Delivering to the Right Audience

When you are listed on our platform, your business starts attracting users that are one step away from making a decision and start spending. The intent each user has when they open our app is to look for something that is nearby and solve their dinner plans. Quick and easy, at the tap of three buttons (sometimes two), the user is provided with a list of highly rated and reviewed restaurants or retail stores.

Nearby Results

Your restaurant or retail store will appear in a list according to its category. Results from this list which you appear in, are drawn according to the user's distance from your store. For example, let's say Sam and Frodo are hanging out on a Friday night near the beach. Suddenly Sam gets hungry and decides he wants to eat something... he knows where to go. All he has to do is tap the SpotBie app and he'll be getting a list of nearby results that are going to offer him a superb experience.
