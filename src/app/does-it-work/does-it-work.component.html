What problems does SpotBie solve?

Loyalty programs are well known to bring in additional revenue, customer retention, and brand awareness for businesses. Many large franchises and retails have in-house loyalty programs. However, some small businesses are left with less options to compete because they lack the funding for maintaining an in-house loyalty program.


Alternatively, some businesses use third party loyalty programs that are integrated with POS systems such as Square. While this is a great option, it leaves out the focus on building a highly rich loyalty program experience for your users because of their broad focus.


To solve these problems, we've built a platform that solely focuses on loyalty programs and all their benefits while staying within an affordable membership for our businesses. By focusing on bringing our users what they are really interested in (savings, rewards, more customers, insights, etc.) we've arrived at a solution for businesses and everyday users that we think you'll like.

Acquisition & Retention

Loyalty programs offer a huge incentive for guests to visit your store. Here at SpotBie, we've added an extra twist that helps bring new customers to your store and retain existing ones.

1. The SpotBie Map

Our app allows users to interactively find businesses that are around them. Once they open the SpotBie app and tap on the category they are searching for, they are presented with a list of nearby businesses. This makes it easy for users to choose a store, nearing the gap between them and gaining a new customer.

2. The SpotBie Algorithm

The SpotBie algorithm periodically adjusts your business' reward ratio on the platform so that you can remain competent amongst other businesses. Users that look at these businesses through our map can then compare reward rates and decide where to go.

3. Different Loyalty Campaigns

To keep things entertaining for your customers, we have included the ability to award items, discounts, and experiences.
