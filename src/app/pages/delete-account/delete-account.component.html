DELETE YOUR ACCOUNT & DATA

Overview...

In this page we'll cover ways in which you can delete your account from the SpotBie platform. When deleting your account you have three options;

1. Permanent Deletion

This is a irreversible action and it means that you will not be able to recover your account. Your loyalty points, rewards, places visited, (e.g., all data associated with your unique account identifier will be permanently deleted). If you want to permanently delete your account, you can do so under the settings section of our website.

2. Temporary Deletion

If for some reason you'd like to delete your SpotBie account temporarily and gain access to it later, you'll be able to do so by simply navigating to settings and tapping on "Deactivate Account" as shown in this screenshot.

3. Delete Account Data

SpotBie also allows users to delete their account data without deleting their account, simply e-mail or call us and we will gladly take care of this for you.
