How to Update Your App

1. Download the app from the current installed version on your Android tablet.

2. Uninstall the current app by going to "Settings" > "Apps" > "SB-Business" > "Uninstall".

3. Navigate to the Downloads folder by opening your Files app in your Android Tablet.

4. Find the SB-Business.apk file you downloaded from the app, it might prompt you to allow installation, answer yes.

5. Once the app is done installing, you can start using the new version.
