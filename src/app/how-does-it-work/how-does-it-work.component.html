HOW DOES IT WORK

Sign Up to Our Platform...

You will be able to set your restaurant's or retail store's location along with your categories, store description, and store name. After you confirm your account, you will be able to upload rewards and start awarding your users.

Local Exposure

Your business will be listed in our platform to users who are ready to spend. SpotBie users open the SpotBie app because they are ready to go to the next step in the sales funnel; and that is making the purchase. It's just a question of where they will go now. We advertise our local SpotBie Community Members at different times and locations. You won't have to worry about managing your ad campaigns with us.

Loyalty Points

Our users claim Loyalty Points by making purchases at SpotBie Community Member locations. SpotBie users can then claim rewards once they have enough points.

Upload Rewards

As the business owner you can upload the best of your menu items onto your digital rewards menu to entice your audience. Each reward you upload can also carry a minimum spend policy (how much users must spend in order to redeem this reward).

In-Platform Advertising

As part of your membership, you will receive a new in-app Ad design every six months. Everyone gets a fair chance for the spot-light in our app. Our algorithm assigns SpotBie Community Members Exposure in a systematic manner so that all of them get the same amount of views.

Redeeming Rewards

Users that go into your business can claim any of the rewards that you've listed on your menu as long as they have the Loyalty Point balance for it. Additionally, users might be required to gain accesss to loyalty tiers before they can redeem certain rewards. This is a great tool for your business to thank your most frequent guests with the more exclusive rewards.

Award Users

Award your guests with Loyalty Points at check-out. Simply enter their bill value and the SpotBie app will generate a QR code for them to scan. After they scan the QR code, the points will be transferred to their account.
